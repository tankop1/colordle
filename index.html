Colordle

The goal of the game is to guess the correct word.

The rules are the same as Wordle, but the colors change every time you play. There is always one color for a correct letter in the correct spot, a correct letter in the incorrect spot, and an incorrect letter, however you have to figure out which color is which.

Also, guesses do not have to be a valid word.

Good Luck!
